Sachin Tendulkar is a former international cricketer from India, widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket.

He was born on April 24, 1973, in Mumbai, India, and made his debut for the Indian national cricket team at the age of 16.

During his illustrious career, Sachin Tendulkar achieved numerous records, including being the highest run-scorer in international cricket and the first player to score 100 international centuries.

He was a key part of the Indian team that won the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and his batting prowess and dedication to the sport have made him a cricketing legend.

Sachin Tendulkar is also known for his philanthropic efforts and contributions to various social causes.
